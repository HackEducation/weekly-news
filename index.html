How to Read My Work Via (Email) Newsletter


Hack Education Weekly Newsletter: I send this newsletter out once a week (on Saturdays, typically). Sent via Tinyletter, this newsletter collects some of the week's most interesting/horrifying links in education and technology. Sign up here.

Hack Education RSS-to-Email: This newsletter, as the name suggests, sends a full copy of all new blog posts from Hack Education to your email inbox. Sent via Mailchimp, this newsletter is sent automatically every time Hack Education is updated. Sign up here.

Audreywatters.com RSS-to-Email: This newsletter, as the name suggests, sends a full copy of all new blog posts from my personal blog to your email inbox. Sent via Mailchimp, this newsletter is sent automatically once a week (on Mondays). Sign up here.

Contrafabulists Newsletter: This newsletter goes out once a week (on Sundays, typically). Sent via Mailchimp, this newsletter provides a link to the weekly Contrafabulists podcast episode, as well as updates on what Kin Lane and I are up to as writers, speakers, and travelers. Sign up here.

I will not sell or share these mailing lists with anyone. Hell, I rarely look at who’s signed up or who’s un-enrolled because I don’t give a damn about analytics.
